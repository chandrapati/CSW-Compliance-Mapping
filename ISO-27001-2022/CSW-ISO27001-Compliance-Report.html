Executive Summary

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 restructured Annex A from 114 controls to 93 controls across four themes: Organizational, People, Physical, and Technological. Cisco Secure Workload (CSW) primarily addresses Technological theme controls (A.8) and contributes significantly to Organizational controls (A.5) around threat intelligence, incident management, and cloud security — including several controls new in the 2022 revision.

CSW's continuous visibility, enforcement, and forensic capabilities enable organizations to achieve and maintain ISO 27001:2022 certification with documented, auditable evidence for each applicable control.
