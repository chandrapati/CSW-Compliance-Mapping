Executive Summary

NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 provides the comprehensive security control catalog for federal information systems and is widely adopted in regulated commercial environments. This report maps Cisco Secure Workload (CSW) capabilities to the seven control families most directly addressed: Access Control (AC), Audit & Accountability (AU), Configuration Management (CM), Incident Response (IR), Risk Assessment (RA), System & Communications Protection (SC), and System & Information Integrity (SI).

CSW achieves Full Coverage across all 18 mapped controls through workload-level policy enforcement, continuous telemetry, vulnerability management, and forensic investigation capabilities.
