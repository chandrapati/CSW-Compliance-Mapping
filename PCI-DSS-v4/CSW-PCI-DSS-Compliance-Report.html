Executive Summary

PCI DSS v4.0 introduces the Customized Approach and strengthens network segmentation requirements significantly. Cisco Secure Workload (CSW) directly satisfies Requirements 1 (Network Security Controls), 6 (Vulnerability Management), 7 (Access Control), 10 (Logging & Monitoring), and 11 (Security Testing) — the five requirements most commonly cited in PCI DSS audit findings.

CSW's workload-level micro-segmentation provides stronger CDE isolation than traditional firewall-only approaches, directly addressing QSA concerns about lateral movement within the cardholder data environment.
